Account Name
What name do you want to use? It does not have to be unique. It will be used as the default name when creating or joining games.
Account Security
Your account is tied to this device and browser. But, you can lose your account if you clear local storage or lose your device. To protect your account or use it on another device, generate a secret link. This link is like a very long password. It can only be used once and expires after 30 days. Keep it someplace safe.
Device List
This is a list of devices tied to your account. You can remove any device except this one. You may click on a device name to customize it. The access details are fairly technical, but can be useful to detect unauthorized access to your account.
