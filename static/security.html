!!Account Recovery
You are at risk of accidentally losing your account!
Linking to an external account allows you to easily recover your account or use it on more than one device.
Generate and share an account link with another device. Otherwise, this device might forget your account and you will have no way to recover it.
Device List
This is a list of devices tied to your account. You can remove any device except this one. You may click on a device name to customize it. The access details are fairly technical, but can be useful to detect unauthorized access to your account.
